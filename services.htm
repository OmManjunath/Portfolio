MODULAR KITCHENS BY OM MANJUNATH INTERIORS

Smart Designs. Quality Build. Personalized for Your Home.

At Om Manjunath Interiors, we believe the kitchen is the heart of every home. It’s where meals are prepared, memories are made, and families come together. Whether you have a compact apartment or a spacious home, our modular kitchen solutions are designed to suit your needs, lifestyle, and budget.

We specialize in practical, durable, and elegant kitchens—crafted for everyday living and built to last.

Straight Kitchen

Perfect for small homes or studio apartments, the Straight Kitchen places all cabinets and appliances along a single wall. It’s an efficient design that offers maximum function with minimum space.

Simple - Sleek - Space-Saving

U-Shaped Kitchen

With counters on three sides, the U-Shaped Kitchen gives you plenty of room to cook, store, and work. It’s perfect for larger spaces and for those who need a highly functional kitchen for daily use.

Spacious - Organized Efficient

L-Shaped Kitchen

Designed using two adjoining walls, the L-Shaped Kitchen makes great use of corner space and fits easily into both small and large rooms. It allows free movement and works well for open-plan layouts.

Flexible - Functional - Family-Friendly

Island Kitchen

An Island Kitchen features a central work surface that can be used for meal prep, dining, or storage. Best suited for open or larger kitchens, this layout brings people together while making your kitchen the highlight of your home.

Modern - Multi Functional - Stylish

Parallel Kitchen

Also known as a corridor kitchen, the Parallel Kitchen has two counters facing each other. It creates a smooth workflow, with separate zones for cooking and washing. Ideal for narrow or medium-sized kitchens.

Compact - Practical - High-Performance

Dry & Wet Kitchen

Separate your light and heavy cooking with Dry & Wet Kitchen Zones.

  • Dry Area: Used for light cooking, snacks, baking, or serving
  • Wet Area: Handles daily cooking, cleaning, and strong aromas

This setup keeps your main area fresh and clean. Especially useful in modern Indian homes.

Clean Cooking - Smart Planning
