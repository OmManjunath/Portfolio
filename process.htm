From Idea to Reality – Made Simple

At Om Manjunath Interiors, we’ve streamlined the interior design experience to make it smooth, clear, and worry-free. Here’s how we work with you, step by step.

Connect with Our Designers

Share your space, needs, style, and budget. We listen carefully and provide a clear, no-obligation estimate.

On-Site Visit

We visit your home to take precise measurements and understand your lifestyle within your space.

Choose Your Materials

We create 2D layouts and 3D designs with finish samples, letting you visualize and refine your dream interior.

Precision Manufacturing

We build your interiors in our advanced facility using top-grade materials and machinery for precision.

Site Readiness & Delivery

After civil work, we deliver your modular kitchens, wardrobes, and furniture safely, on time.

Expert Installation

Our trained installers ensure perfect fit, finish, and functionality in your new interiors.

Final Checks & Handover

We conduct quality checks to ensure everything is perfect before handing over your space.

Final Quote & Approval

We provide a detailed quote with finalized designs and material lists for your approval before we begin.
